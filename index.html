My Favorite Movies

Title: Mulan
Director: Niki Caro
Release date: July 24, 2020
Synopsis: A fearless young woman disguises herself as a man and battles northern invaders in China in order to keep her unwell father from serving in the Imperial Army.

Title: Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald
Director: David Yates
Release date: November 16, 2018
Synopsis: In an effort to thwart Grindelwald's plans of raising pure-blood wizards to rule over all non-magical beings, Albus Dumbledore enlists his former student. Although he agrees to help, he is unaware of the dangers that lie ahead.

Title: Kung Fu Panda 3
Directors: Jennifer Yuh Nelson and Alessandro Carloni
Release date: January 29, 2016
Synopsis: Living large and loving life, Po realizes that he has a lot to learn if he is going to fulfill the next challenge from his beloved instructor. After reuniting with his long-lost father, Po must train a group of pandas. Together, they will be able to take on a supernatural warrior known as Kai.

Title: Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – The Movie: Mugen Train
Director: Haruo Sotozaki
Release date: April 23, 2021
Synopsis: Three demon slayers go on a mission which is to defeat a demon who has been tormenting people and killing the demon slayers who oppose it.

Title: Howl's Moving Castle
Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Release date: June 17, 2005
Synopsis: Sophie has an uneventful life at her late father's hat shop. All of that changes after she befriends a certain wizard named Howl, who lives in a magical flying castle. However, an evil witch takes issue with their budding relationship and casts a spell on young Sophie, making her age prematurely. Now, Howl must use all his magical talents to battle the jealous hag and return Sophie to her former youth and beauty.
